Privacy Policy

We collect information directly from you through the data you provide us with when you access our website. We record your location, name, email id, phone number, log in details, IP address, and other information you may provide.

Location: We collect the precise location your device is operating from when you access our website from your system. When you permit the Elite Fit website/app to access your location through the permission settings of your device, we collect both precise and tentative location from your IP address to record data for optimizing our services according to your preference.

Contacts: We automatically gain access to your name, email address, and phone number when you choose to sign up on our website. Additionally, we also gain access to your phonebook if you permit Elite Fit app/website through your mobile device. We record and store this information to improve the visibility of our services.

Preference and use: When you or the other visitors use our website, we collect information on usage patterns and how you interact with others on our website. This helps us understand your terms of use and tweak our services and website layouts to better serve and suit your preferences.

Device information: We collect your device information including your software version, hardware model, operating system, unique device identifier, language preference and your mobile network system.

Call and SMS: Our website tracks and keeps a record of your calls and SMS including the time of sending/receiving the messages and calls, and contents of the SMS.

Activity data: When you access our website/app, we may collect information about your device activities such as pages visited, access date and time, IP address, browser type, the type of website you were interacting with before accessing our service page.

Terms of Usage

We collect information about you to:

  • Improve data functionality, our services, upgrade our features, provide better support to our customers, authenticate users, maintain safety features;
  • Personalize our services and tailor make our web flow according to your preferences;
  • Communicate to you through email and text messages to keep you updated on our services, news, new openings, and about anything that may be of interest to you.
  • Track and remove bug errors, improve internal operations, eradicate potential bugs, analyze data points, and other activity trends

Sharing of Information

We share your information in these cases:

  • With any third party we interact with as a part of our offerings; to provide you with integrated service/information
  • On public platforms when you choose to leave comment(s) on our website blogs, social media content, etc.
  • If a competent authority raises concerns and stands eligible to be disclosed data for legal purposes.
