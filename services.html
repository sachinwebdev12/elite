Our Luxury Amenities

Ice Bath

An immersive experience in icy water yields multiple health benefits. From reducing inflammation and swelling to enhancing physical and mental function and improving blood circulation, our ice baths provide a refreshing and revitalizing experience. Our ice baths are exposed to just the right amount of ‘chill,’ making it into a relaxing recreation.

Sauna

The sauna tradition dates back centuries, with cultures worldwide benefiting from the therapeutic effects of warm steam. At Elite Fit, our wellness philosophy emphasizes physical endurance and mental relaxation, enhancing overall physical function while promoting inner peace.

Normatec

Normatec mimics natural contractions, aiding in relaxing muscular stiffness. As a versatile recovery device, athletes and fitness enthusiasts use it regularly to alleviate muscle pain and enhance blood flow. Achieve faster recovery and maintain strength with Normatec.
